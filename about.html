ABOUT

I am an independent researcher aiming to achieve AI capable of conducting academic research. I am currently engaged in research and development aimed at creating AI capable of conducting AI research.

I am also interested in "metascience"; exploring the nature of science itself, including alternative approaches to science and more optimized knowledge production, as well as creating environments where researchers can conduct their work in a manner that supports their mental and physical well-being.

I completed my master of research at the Department of Complexity Science and Engineering at the University of Tokyo, where I researched neural network learning dynamics. Prior to that, I received my B.A. in Economics at Keio University, where I studied economics, international relations, and neuroaesthetics.
