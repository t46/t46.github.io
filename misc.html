ABOUT RESEARCH MISC

PROJECTS

I am assisting with a project lead by Wataru Kumagai called "AutoRes", which aims to automate machine learning research. AutoRes is engaged in developing human-in-the-loop research assistants, systems that automatically conduct experiments without human intervention, and research on automated theorem proving.


Additionally, I support the development of decentralized science through "DeSci.Tokyo" lead by Hiro Taiyo Hamada. DeSci.Tokyo hosted an event called DeSci.Tokyo Conference 2023 in 2023 and co-organize an event called Funding the Commons Tokyo in 2024.

PODCAST

I'm a member of a Japanese podcast lead by Hiro Taiyo Hamada called "metascientia," where we discuss science from a meta-perspective. In the past, we've covered topics like university history, research funding, and research impact assessment.
