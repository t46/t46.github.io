About

I am an independent researcher. My long-term research goal is to create an artificial researcher. I'm currently writing a perspective paper towards general and autonomous artificial researcher (in progress). I'm planning to publish it on arXiv in autumn.

I became an independent researcher to contribute to developing research ecosystems outside the existing system. I'd love to discuss with metascience entrepreneurs* sharing the vision with me.

I completed my master of research at the Department of Complexity Science and Engineering at the University of Tokyo. Prior to that, I received my B.A. in Economics at Keio University, where I studied economics, international relations, and neuroaesthetics.

* Michael Nielsen and Kanjun Qiu, 2022, "A Vision of Metascience"

Research

I'm now working on studies for realizing general and autonomous artificial researcher. In the past I have been involved in research on the following topics:
  • Pre-Training in NLP
  • Offile RL
  • NNGP (Neural Network Gaussian Process)
  • Topological data analysis
  • Continual learning
  • Meta-learning
  • Out-of-distribution genralization
  • Trainability of neural networks
  • Disentangled representation
  • Statistical mechanical analysis of learning dynamics of neural networks
  • Sense of agency
